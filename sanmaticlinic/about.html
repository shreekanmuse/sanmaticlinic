Dr. Smeeta B. Kanmuse

M.D. (Ayurveda), M.Sc. (Yoga)

Professor in Samhita & Ayurvedic Physician

Academic & Clinical Leader

Nurturing Generations of Physicians, Healing Lives

Dr. Smeeta B. Kanmuse is an esteemed and highly accomplished Ayurvedic physician with over 20 years of clinical expertise and a deep commitment to holistic health. Holding an M.D. in Ayurveda and an M.Sc. in Yoga, she seamlessly blends the ancient wisdom of traditional Indian medicine, purification therapies, and yogic sciences to guide patients toward lasting recovery and optimal vitality.

Since 2003, Dr. Kanmuse has served as a distinguished faculty member at the prestigious Hingulambika Ayurvedic Medical College in Kalaburagi, nurturing the next generation of Ayurvedic physicians while contributing significantly to traditional wellness research. Her clinical approach combines detailed pulse diagnostic techniques (Nadi Pariksha) with personalized herbal formulations and lifestyle counseling, making her a trusted authority in holistic healthcare.

Our Mission (Seva)

To offer dedicated service (Seva) and holistic healing therapies using pure, organic herbal solutions and classical Ayurveda principles to cure chronic conditions from the root. We are committed to ethical treatment paths and maintaining transparent diagnostic practices.

Our Vision (Swasthya)

To make true physical, mental, and social health (Swasthya) accessible by preserving cultural ethics (Sanskar) and translating Ayurvedic diagnostics into actionable wellness lifestyle habits. We aim to establish a sanctuary of absolute calm where every therapy is a sacred journey.
